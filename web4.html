Web 4.0 - The Intelligent Web

What is Web 4.0?

Web 4.0 is the future vision of the internet, sometimes called the "intelligent web." It combines the internet with artificial intelligence (AI) and smart physical devices (Internet of Things). The idea is that the web will not just respond to requests but will intelligently understand context and anticipate what users need — blending the digital and physical worlds together.

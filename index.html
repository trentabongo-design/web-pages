About This Website

This website was built to break down the key terms and concepts that form the backbone of the modern internet and the World Wide Web. Whether you are new to the topic or just need a solid reference, each page here goes into detail on one specific term — explaining what it is, how it works, and why it matters.

Use the navigation bar at the top to jump to any term at any time. Every page is self-contained and links back to all others, so you can explore in any order that suits you.

Extranet

What is an Extranet?

An extranet is a private network that extends an organisation's intranet to selected outside users such as suppliers, clients, or business partners. It gives those specific people limited access to part of the internal network, while keeping everything else private from the public.

Comparison

  • Internet — open to everyone
  • Intranet — only people inside the organisation
  • Extranet — organisation + selected trusted outsiders

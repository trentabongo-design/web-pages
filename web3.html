Web 3.0 - The Decentralised Web

What is Web 3.0?

Web 3.0 is the next evolution of the internet, currently still being developed. It focuses on decentralisation — meaning no single company controls it. It uses blockchain technology to store data across many computers rather than in one central place, giving users more ownership and control over their own data.
